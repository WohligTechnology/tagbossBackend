About Us

Innergize Solutions Private Limited is a strategy and management consultancy firm that provides a broad spectrum of services including execution support to diverse clientele that ranges from Indian corporations, multinationals, mid-sized and emerging businesses and even high net worth individuals.

Our approach is to deliver and sustain tangible competitive advantages for our clients through value-added consulting backed up with tangible support. Our advisory services adapt to the climate of the client's industry to provide significant and measurable improvements in the output, efficiency and performance.

The pillars of our service philosophy are substantial industry experience, knowledge of leading practices and a firm grip on business and governance processes. We have the ability to mobilize the right people, skill sets and expertise across functional areas to deliver results for our clients.

Innergize is guided by the extraordinary vision of our CEO, Nilesh Ganjwala, a veteran financial consultant with over 30 years of experience, which is evident in his insights on strategy, finance, taxation and corporate best practices.

Nilesh has had several successful stints in the consulting arena as well as industry, accumulating significant experience in growth strategy, mergers and acquisitions, and joint ventures for major players across different sectors. Combined with expertise in consulting, investment banking, capital and financial structuring, valuations, due diligence, negotiations, legal implications, intellectual property and regulatory matters, Nilesh has been instrumental in developing Innergize into a practice that provides holistic and unparalleled solutions.
